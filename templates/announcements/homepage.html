Welcome to Judge Announcements! This is the configuration site for a bot that allows you to subscribe to many types of announcements related to the Magic: the Gathering Judge Program. You can log in using your JudgeApps account at the top right of this screen, and then link a Slack or Discord instance that you want to get announcements at. Then, you'll be able to choose what types of announcements (specific JudgeApps Forums, Judge Blogs, and more!) you want to see.

This is in - we'll call it an Open Beta. If you're interested, you're welcome to test it and let us know what you think! Please send your suggestions to our GitHub site, rather than to any other place. You probably want to log in (top right) and then try to connect or configure a Slack instance (left side menu).
